MOTIVATION FACTOR TO ONLINE EDUCATION

MY LIFE GOAL IS TO: Get a better grade and score in the upcoming exams even during this MCO.

Motivation Factor
1. Save time
2. Full of resources
3. Interest
4. Empowerment
5. Caring

Explaination

1. Save time: it mean that student don't need to go out from house to go class. they just need to use internet at home.

2. Full of resources: online education is full of resources because at internet all the information needed have at the internet. they no longer need to go library to find book to find information needed.

3. Interest: most of the student this days love using internet and this help student that do not need a lot of time to read a book but just the important content only.

4. Empowerment: they will feel free to do their task or learning and this will give student choices.

5. Caring: student need to feel that the intructor care that they learn.
